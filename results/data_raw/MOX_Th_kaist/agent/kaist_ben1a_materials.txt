DERIVATION NOTES
================================================================================
Number densities are derived from the mass densities given in the KAIST
PDF using natural isotopic compositions and the following formula:

    N_i = (rho_material * w_i * N_A) / M_i

where:
    rho_material = mass density of the bulk material [g/cm^3]
    w_i          = mass fraction of nuclide i
    N_A          = Avogadro's number  6.02214076E+23 [1/mol]
    M_i          = atomic weight of nuclide i [g/mol]

The U and Pu isotopic vectors used:

    UO2 / MOX Pu vector (relative to total Pu mass):
        Pu-238 :  1.83 w/o
        Pu-239 : 57.93 w/o
        Pu-240 : 22.50 w/o
        Pu-241 : 11.06 w/o
        Pu-242 :  5.60 w/o
        Am-241 :  1.08 w/o    (separately tracked in HM)
    (Derived from UO2 PWR fuel of 33 000 MWd/t burnup, reprocessed
     after 3-yr cooling and 2-yr storage.)

    Gd isotopic vector (natural, w/o of total Gd):
